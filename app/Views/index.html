  • Kids Programs

For the little ones, we offer three levels designed to foster a love of padel from an early age.

  • Mini-Padel (4-6 years): An introduction to the sport, combining play with learning through soft balls and mini-rackets.
  • Kids Padel (7-10 years): Focused on developing technical skills with an emphasis on fun and motivation.
  • Kids Padel (11-14 years): Perfecting technique and learning to apply basic strategies in real matches.

For adults, we offer four levels designed to cater to both beginners and advanced players.

  • Beginners: Ideal for those just starting out. Focus on the fundamentals of holding the paddle and basic strokes.
  • Bronze: Improving on fundamentals with an introduction to tactical play.
  • Silver: Taking your skills to the next level with advanced techniques and strategies.
  • Gold: Designed for high-performance players aiming to refine advanced techniques and strategies.

Our competition programs offer tailored training that emphasizes technical, tactical, and physical development.

  • Physical Preparation: 3 to 5 days per week, depending on age group.
  • Match Analysis: Analyzing games to improve tactical understanding.
  • Nutrition: Specialized nutrition plans starting at the age of 4.
  • Theory: 2-3 hours of game theory per week.
  • Match analysis : Analyze matches to better understand the tactics when playing the matches himself.
  • Nutrition : You can start having controlled and competition-oriented nutrition from 4 years of age.

PADEL COACH CERTIFICATION

Becoming a padel instructor is an excellent opportunity for those passionate about the sport who wish to share their knowledge and skills with others. A padel instructor course typically covers a range of important topics, from technique and tactics to pedagogy and effective communication.

You will learn how to plan and organize training sessions tailored to different skill levels, as well as how to identify and correct common player errors. Additionally, these courses include aspects of group management and motivation, essential for creating a positive learning environment. Upon completion of the course, you will be not only certified but also prepared to inspire and guide others on their path to padel mastery.
